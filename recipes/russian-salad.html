Russian Salad

Description

Russian salad is a popular dish in Russia, consisting of a mix of vegetables and mayonnaise. It is often served as a side dish or as a main course.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Boil the carrots and potatoes until they are tender.
  2. Chop the vegetables into small pieces.
  3. Mix the vegetables with mayonnaise.
  4. Enjoy!
